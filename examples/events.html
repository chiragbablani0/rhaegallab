HACKING WITH AMAZON ALEXA

We have conducted Hacking with Amazon Alexa workshop in association with MLH Local Host continuously for the past two years.

During this workshop participants were taught the basics of how to build skills for Amazon Alexa to run on Alexa devices like the Amazon Tap or Echo.
